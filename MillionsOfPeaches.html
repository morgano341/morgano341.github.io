Palisade Peaches

Palisade Peaches come from the heart of Colorado in Palisade. Near grand junction this town is an amazing place to get the best Peaches in Colorado.
"Locals will tell you Palisade peaches are the best, simply because they're ripe, sweet and loaded with chin-dripping juice," according to the Grand Junction Visitor and Convention Bureau."The Grand Valley's hot days and cool nights are perfect for bringing out the natural sugars in the peaches and growing big fruit.

There are many types of juicy peaches from Colorado, just to name a few there are:

  • Newhaven
  • Earlistar
  • Rising Star
  • PF-7
  • PF-7A
  • Blazing Star
